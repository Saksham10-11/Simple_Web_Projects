Pyaaz Ke Pakore

Description

Pyaz ke pakode with green chutney and tea / chai – perfect tea time snack !! Spicy and crispy these pyaz ke pakore are perfect for that rainy day or as a side dish to any boring khichdi or puri.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Besan (Gram Flour)
  • 2 tblsp Rice flour
  • 2 – 3 medium Onions sliced thinly
  • 2 tblsp Hara Dhania (Coriander Leaves)
  • 10 – 15 Curry Leaves
  • 5 – 6 Green Chilies finely chopped
  • 1 ” crushed Ginger
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Jeera (Cumin Seeds)
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Soda
  • 5 – 7 tsp Water as needed to make batter
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a wide bowl besan, rice flour, baking soda. Mix well.
  2. Now add the rest of ingredients Except water. Add salt , chilli powder, cumin seeds, hara dhania, onions sliced , curry leaves, coarse ginger, chopped green chilies.
  3. With the help of a spoon mix these ingredients with No Water first Then add water little by little until all the onions slices are covered with the dough. It should be thick batter.
  4. Now in a kadai heat oil for frying. On medium heat and in small batches deep fry the onion pakora until they are cooked on both sides.
  5. Do not overcrowd the kadai in one go. Make the pakoras in bacthes.
  6. Crispy pyaz ke pakore are ready to be served.
