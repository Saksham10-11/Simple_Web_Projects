How to Play ?

Tic-tac-toe is a classic game played on a 3x3 grid where two players take turns marking empty squares with either an "X" or an "O". The objective is to get three of your symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, before your opponent. The game ends when one player achieves this goal or when all squares are filled, resulting in a draw. It's a game of strategy and anticipation, with the first player typically choosing "X" and the second "O".
